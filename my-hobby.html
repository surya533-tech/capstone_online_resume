My Hobby

  • Listening to Music

    I love listening to soft and soothing music. It unwinds my mind and gives me a sense of peace. More modern Nepali
    and Hindi Bollywood songs are my favorite. I love classic Narayan Gopal and Bhakta Raj Achaya's songs; they are my
    all-time favorite singers. I also admire modern singers like Arijit Singh and Atif Aslam. But my love for songs
    by Maestro Ghulam Ali and Bhajan Samrat Jagjit singh is eternal.

  • Watching Comedy Videos

    Watching comedy videos is another thing I enjoy the most. Stand-up comedy is my favorite.
    I watch English, Hindi and Nepali comedy videos, those are the only languages I understand.
    I wish I could watch Chinese and Korean comedy videos as well.

    Trevor Noah's Inian Food Comedy

  • Cooking

    • Cooking is something I learned when I was just a teenager. My parents would go to farm, they didn't have
    enough time to cook, so I had to learn how to cook. Slowly, I started to enjoy cooking because I saw
    my parents enjoying the food I cooked.

    These days I have learnt more new recipes on chicken, mutton and fish. When I cook I put my heart and soul into it, and the food turns out to be delicious. I cook Nepali and Indian dishes with
    spices in good balance. I also watch Korean and Chinese cooking videos. They look healthy and delicious.
